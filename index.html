Lovely Box is a gift box filled with encouraging word of scripture, prayer cards, themed trinkets, beauty samples, jewelry, etc...lovely things that will give Hope, Peace, Joy, and a little bit of Beauty to your month! This is also a unique way to provide a monthly subscription to a sister, mother, or a special friend to let her know that she is loved and LOVELY!

LovelyBox's purpose is to encourage, strengthen and empower the Christian Woman and to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ! Join us at LovelyBox.org

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is LovelyBox a Christian company?

A: Yes! Our founders, Ruth Galdamez and Andrea Portilla, both work diligently in their churches in women's ministry. They are a mother/daughter team!

Q: What is LovelyBox and how does it work?

A: Lovely Box was inspired as both women began to see a need in the lives of their friends and coworkers. Christian women that have a full plate and at times feel alone, depressed and lost in a sea of fear. How great would it be to encourage one woman at a time with a LovelyBoxfilled with love?

Q: When will I receive my first box?

A: Upon your subscription we will send your box that same week. You will then receive your monthly box before the 15th of each month thereafter.

Q: When is my credit card charged?

A: Your credit card is charged on the day you sign up; and afterwards, it is rebilled on the first of the month based on your subscription length.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: Not at this time.
